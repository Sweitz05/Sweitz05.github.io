Like an old house on a hill, here we haunt.

Street blares but behind the closed window we strum.

Jack o' lantern ne'er sleeps.

Best laid plans of skeletons.

Bespirited.

Eat more brains.

Neighbors quake. Ignored.

A dishonest invitation.

The vampire waits for the lights to dim to feast. They don't.

Touch the painting if ye dare.

Never a fly on the wall has seen us.

For we cannot carve pumpkins of our own.
